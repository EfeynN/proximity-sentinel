# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
Proximity Sentinel is a security-focused project built on professionalism,
responsibility, and mutual respect.

We are committed to providing a welcoming, harassment-free environment
for everyone involved in the project, regardless of experience level,
background, or role.

---

## Expected Behavior
All contributors, maintainers, and participants are expected to:

- Communicate respectfully and professionally
- Be constructive in feedback and discussions
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences
- Focus on the project’s goals and security standards
- Act in good faith when reporting issues or proposing changes

---

## Unacceptable Behavior
The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language
- Personal attacks, insults, or threats
- Trolling or intentionally disruptive behavior
- Sharing private or sensitive information without consent
- Attempts to bypass, weaken, or sabotage security mechanisms
- Promoting cheating, exploitation, or misuse of the system

---

## Security-Specific Conduct
Because Proximity Sentinel is a security-critical system:

- Vulnerabilities must be reported responsibly as described in `SECURITY.md`
- Public disclosure of sensitive security details is prohibited
- Reverse engineering or exploit discussion outside permitted contexts is not allowed
- Malicious contributions will result in immediate removal

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct.

They may take any action deemed appropriate, including:
- Warning the offending party
- Removing comments, issues, or pull requests
- Temporarily or permanently banning contributors
- Reporting serious misconduct to platform administrators

All enforcement decisions are made with the project’s security and integrity in mind.

---

## Reporting Violations
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

- Contact a project maintainer directly
- For security-related issues, follow the process in `SECURITY.md`

Reports will be reviewed discreetly and handled with care.

---

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including:
- GitHub issues and pull requests
- Discussions and comments
- Any official communication channels related to Proximity Sentinel

---

## Legal Notice
Participation in this project implies agreement with this Code of Conduct.

Violations may result in loss of access to the project and related resources.
All actions must comply with applicable laws and platform policies.

---

## Acknowledgement
This Code of Conduct is adapted to fit the needs of a security-sensitive project
and is inspired by industry best practices for responsible collaboration.
